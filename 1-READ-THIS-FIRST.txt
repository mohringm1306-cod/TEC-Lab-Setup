============================================================
  TEC Lab Remote Desktop - SETUP INSTRUCTIONS
  College of DuPage
============================================================

*** FIRST: EXTRACT THIS ZIP FILE! ***

You MUST extract all files before running anything:
1. Right-click the zip file
2. Select "Extract All..."
3. Click "Extract"
4. Open the extracted folder

The batch file will NOT work from inside the zip!

============================================================

IMPORTANT: Complete these steps IN ORDER!

============================================================
STEP 1: SET UP MICROSOFT AUTHENTICATOR ON YOUR PHONE
============================================================
You MUST do this BEFORE running the installer!

1. On your PHONE:
   - Install "Microsoft Authenticator" from App Store or Play Store
   - Make sure it says "Microsoft Corporation" as the developer

2. On your COMPUTER, go to:
   https://mysignins.microsoft.com/security-info

3. Sign in with your COD email (yourname@dupage.edu)

4. Click "+ Add sign-in method"

5. Select "Authenticator app" from the dropdown, click "Add"

6. Follow the prompts:
   - Click "Next" on the "Start by getting the app" screen
   - Click "Next" on the "Set up your account" screen
   - On your PHONE, tap "Scan a QR code" in the Authenticator app
   - Scan the QR code shown on your computer screen
   - Tap "Approve" on the notification that appears on your phone
   - Click "Next" on your computer when it says "Notification approved"

7. NOW THE CRITICAL STEP - Set your default sign-in method:
   - Click "Change" next to "Default sign-in method"
   - Select "App based authentication - notification"

   *** DO NOT SELECT "code" - HP Anyware will NOT work! ***

============================================================
STEP 2: RUN THE INSTALLER (Windows Only)
============================================================
After completing Step 1, double-click:

   2-Run-This-To-Connect.bat

This will:
- Install HP Anyware if needed (click Yes on any prompts)
- Configure the connection settings automatically
- Connect you to TEC Lab

If you see a SmartScreen warning:
- Click "More info"
- Click "Run anyway"

============================================================
STEP 3: LOG IN TO TEC LAB
============================================================
1. Enter your COD username (just username, NOT the full email)
   Example: smithj (not smithj@dupage.edu)

2. Enter your COD password

3. Click "Connect"

4. Click "Request Push"

5. On your phone, tap "Approve" on the notification

6. Select an available computer from the list

============================================================
NEXT TIME
============================================================
After the first setup, a "TEC Lab" shortcut is created on
your desktop. Just double-click it to reconnect.

============================================================
TROUBLESHOOTING
============================================================
"Request Push" doesn't work:
  -> Your default sign-in must be "notification" not "code"
  -> Go to mysignins.microsoft.com/security-info and change it

SmartScreen blocks the installer:
  -> Click "More info" then "Run anyway"

Installation fails:
  -> Make sure you have internet access
  -> Click "Yes" on administrator prompts

Can't connect:
  -> Check your internet connection
  -> On campus? Connect to COD-WiFi or use ethernet
